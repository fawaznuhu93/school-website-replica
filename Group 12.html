About Us

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

The Confluence University of Science and Technology (CUSTECH), Osara is a State Government owned University in Nigeria. It was established on 20th August 2020. Its establishment aims to give effect to the Nation's drive for much-needed self-reliance in Science, Engeering and Technology.

Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and innovative curriculum ensure a comprehensive educational experience that prepares students not just for univeristy, but for life as a whole.
